Tail Burner Firehouse French Fries

Description

Fries with spice! Derived from firefighters! Play with the amount of seasonings to best suit your taste and tolerance for heat!

Ingredients

  • 1 (32 ounce) package frozen French fries
  • 2 ½ tablespoons lemon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons dried red pepper seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • black pepper to taste
  • 2 pinches chili powder
  • ¼ cup butter
